Here are some of my recent works that showcase my design and development skills.

Auditable LLM Assistant for Colombian Traffic & Transport Law (Ongoing Thesis)

An auditable RAG-based assistant for Colombian Traffic & Transport Law in Colombia designed to support legal professionals with reproducible, citation-grounded answers. The system retrieves exclusively from national legal sources, chunks content by article with metadata, and returns structured JSON outputs for traceability. It includes an evaluation harness focused on citation fidelity, faithfulness, parameter extraction accuracy, refusal quality, and robustness under paraphrase and jurisdiction-leakage checks. Deliverables include a curated legal corpus, a gold Q&A set, and a Legal Transparency Card to document limitations and governance.

RAG Responsible xAI Legal LLM Assistant JSON-Schema

Colgate-Palmolive Mock WhatsApp Chatbot

An end‑to‑end RAG system that delivers fast, grounded answers tailored to Colgate’s brand and content. It scrapes the company's official pages, social media content, and product listings in Colombia. Data is cleaned, chunked, and embedded to support an integrated WhatsApp chatbot using N8N as the automation layer. After analyzing model cost and usage efficiency, the system was optimized for Gemini, balancing performance with scalability. It includes scraping pipelines (Selenium/BeautifulSoup), custom prompt templates with safety guardrails, and structured response formatting.

LangChain BeautifulSoup Selenium GeminiAPI

Child Face Censoring System

A privacy-focused vision pipeline using YOLOv8 and OpenCV to detect and blur children’s faces in photos, videos, and live feeds. Designed for ethical and legal compliance, it processes each frame before display or export. The project involved rigorous data labeling and iterative testing to ensure the model balanced privacy with usability (accurately protecting children’s identities without excessive false positives). Modular and lightweight, the system is optimized for low-resource environments and adaptable to other anonymization tasks.

PyTorch YOLOv8 OpenCV Roboflow

AI-Driven Biogas System for Coffee Waste Management Proposal

Contributed to a research project submitted to MinCiencias that proposed using artificial intelligence to operate biodigesters which transform coffee waste, a major source of water pollution, into clean biogas for rural communities. My work focused on researching multiagent systems to autonomously manage the biodigesters and on designing a chatbot that explains the system and provides real time guidance based on IoT data. I also helped develop the project’s long term sustainability strategy, emphasizing environmental impact, autonomy, and community engagement.

IoT Sensors Multi-Agent Systems Sustainable Development Circular Bioenergy

Skillr - A Sports Skill Sharing Social Media Platform

A prototype of a social media platform that empowers users to showcase and teach their sporting abilities through short video clips tagged by discipline and skill level. The platform supports media uploads, tag-based discovery, and real-time feed updates with features like comments and likes, simulating the dynamic experience of leading social platforms. I led front-end development and UI logic, ensuring smooth clip rendering, consistent tagging, and responsive design across devices. The platform was enhanced with semantic HTML and basic SEO for realistic deployment performance.

HTML5/CSS3 JavaScript Firestore SEO Techniques
